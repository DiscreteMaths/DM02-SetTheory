\item Law 1 : Multiplcation of Logarithms

\item Law 2 : Division of Logarithms
\[ \frac{Log(a)}{Log(b)} = Log(a-b) \]
\item Law 3 : : Powers of Logarithms
\[ Log(a^b) = b \times Log(a) \]
\end{itemize}
